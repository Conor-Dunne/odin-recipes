Lemon Water

Description

Water comes from space; so do lemons. Enjoy the wonders of space with this cool recipe

Ingredients

  • water
  • lemon

Steps

  1. With a karate-chopping motion, quarter the lemon
  2. Pour water into a glass in whatever manner you like
  3. Drop in a lemon wegde
  4. Wait a minute
  5. Sip!
