Cheese on Toast

Description

This ancient dish from the early Martian Invasion period dates back to over 1.7 trillion light-years when the first alien invaders became hungry and stole some bread and cheese from the rebel farmers. Originally, the cheese was melted by atomic-beam rays, but nowadays it is common to use an everyday grill.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • sub-atomic salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Find local rebels with fresh bread
  2. Destroy them and steal their bread
  3. Take their cheese too
  4. Put cheese on bread
  5. Either grill or ray blast everything until golden brown
  6. Add dash of sub-atomic salt (optional)
